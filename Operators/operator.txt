Operator in js
1. Arthmetic Operator ->  ( +, -, *, / ), Modulus(%) Exponentiation 
Eg: a+b (here a and b is Operand & + is the Operator)

2. Unary Operator -> Increment(++), Decrement(--)

3. Assignment Operators -> To assign value ( = , += , -= , *=, /= )

4. Comparison Operators
Equal to (==)
Equal to & type (===)
Not equal to(!=)
Not equal to & type(!==)
(>,>=,<,<=)

5. Logical Operator 
Logical AND (&&)
Logical OR (||)
Logical NOT (!)
